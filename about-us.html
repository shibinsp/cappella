What We Do

Cappella operates across three core strategies, each designed to expand access to quality education while building a resilient, income-generating real-estate portfolio.

01

PropCo + OpCo

Complete takeover of school operations alongside the underlying infrastructure.

02

Sale & Leaseback

Acquiring school infrastructure (land and buildings) and leasing it back to the operator.

03

Greenfield + School District

Developing end-to-end, innovative edu-infra products in collaboration with operators, productised through SKOLEN, our spec-school platform.

SKOLEN

Greenfield, modular school infrastructure

Across India, schools are too often developed as one-off, custom projects: years spent acquiring land, designing and constructing before a single classroom opens. SKOLEN rethinks this approach with standardised, pre-designed campuses that meet the needs of the vast majority of K-12 operators and can be operational without the extended timelines of traditional builds.
